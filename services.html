Services

our services

  • Full Kitchen or Bedroom Refurbishment

    • Our full design and refurbishment service is flexible enough and adaptable to suit all types of projects. We work tirelessly to ensure that your contract is attended to professionally and that it runs smoothly right through to the end. Our design and costing team will listen attentively to what you want and then, based upon your budget, work with you to select the ideal refurbishment package for you.

      Whatever the size of your space, inside or outside, we will tailor our service and our products to match. Our goal is threefold; to provide you with a truly professional team; to make expert decisions; and to communicate effectively and transparently at all times. Whether you are looking for a total home interior/exterior property refurbishment or maybe a kitchen or bathroom transformation, we are here to carry it out expertly.

  • Supply only service

    • You tell us your requirements and we will manufacture to your specifications, again all made to measure. We can supply just replacement doors or complete wardrobes, drawers and accessories. Free standing furniture is also an option.

      Delivery is included and your furniture can be with you within four weeks from you placing your order. Choose from any of our 30+ colours, numerous designs and huge choice of handles... For a FREE quotation, just bring in or e-mail* your requirements to us. It couldn’t be easier and the savings can be really worth while.

  • Kitchen / Bedroom Facelift

    • If your cabinets are in good condition you may choose to facelift your kitchen, we can replace door and drawer fronts, handles, worktops, cornice, pelmets and plinths. You may want a new sink and tap or appliances or you may want to add some extra cabinets . Whether you choose to replace just your doors or go for a complete facelift it will still be considerably cheaper than a complete new kitchen.

      And remember all our vinyl wrapped, Acrylic & Painted doors are made to measure so you can have any door in any size in any finish option. We will help you plan your kitchen face lift and all work is carried out by our experienced fitters. Whether you’re looking for a contemporary on-trend look or a more traditional design, we can work with you and advise you on how to achieve your desired look.
